ABOUT ME

Hi I'm TIMEA TOTH I was born and raised in HUNGARY, a city that has given me the opportunity to work as a beauty and make-up artist since 2002. From 2005 to 2009 I was a freelance beauty therapist and makeup artist in Budapest.

Currently I live in Wellington New Zealand. I work as professional beauty and makeup artist and here you can see part of my portfolio.

I get booked for various events, film production and fashion shows as well as commercial and beauty photo shoots. Available for long term client relationships. I'm always open for interesting projects. Feel free to contact me. Since the beginning of my career I prefer to work with quality products. Positive feedbacks from customers and my professional experience have convinced me of these products. Quality, progress and passion for perfection speak for themselves and are matters very dear to me.

I think in makeup there are no rules. When I was a young girl I always fantasised about red lips and beautiful silky skin. From there I knew what I wanted to be.

I'm not afraid of the unknown. I'm never satisfied with half measures. The trials and "errors" I have learned often led me to more exciting discoveries.

I have found the words "never" and "always" are a sure death to creativity. I always try to stay open for endless combinations and endless possibilities.
